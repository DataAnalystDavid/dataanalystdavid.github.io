Sales/Customers Interactive Dashboards

(Kindly view on tableau to interact with these dashboards.)

This project showcases interactive Tableau dashboards designed to monitor year-over-year trends in customer behavior, sales performance, and overall business growth. It visualizes and compares key performance metrics such as customer retention, purchasing frequency, and revenue fluctuations across multiple years.

By analyzing changes in customer engagement and sales volume over time, the dashboards uncover valuable patterns such as seasonal purchasing habits, shifts in customer loyalty, and emerging revenue opportunities. These insights empower businesses to make valuable decisions in areas like marketing strategy, sales planning, and personalized customer outreach.
